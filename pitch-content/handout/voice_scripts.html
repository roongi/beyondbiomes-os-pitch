⭐ Master 3-min pitch — v3 recommended

Nature → AI-native. The "Witness" arc. ~480 words · 3:00. Mapped to the locked 12 slides.
1 · TITLE

What if the most advanced AI we're building already had a 3.5-billion-year-old blueprint? I'm Dr. Ringo Schwabe — a microbiologist. Everyone in AI is racing to build stronger muscles, and Gartner says over forty percent of those agent projects will be cancelled by 2027 — because they're blind. Nature never made that mistake: it built the eyes before the muscles. Our rule is one line: read the system before you change it.

2 · PROBLEM

Take the oldest system we farm — the soil. We still read a living microbiome as flat chemistry: nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium. That's like debugging software by weighing the hardware — you learn what's there, never what's missing. So billions in biological products get applied blind.

3 · LANDSCAPE

Three people stand around every field who've never been in the same conversation. The farmer, who knows his land by hand but can't see the biology beneath it. The scientist, who knows what's failing but is nowhere near the field. The producer, who sells the cure and hopes it survives.

4 · TECHNOLOGY

Beyond Biomes connects them — by borrowing nature's oldest trick. In a living cell, the enzyme that copies DNA never grades its own work; a separate proofreader checks it. Nature decided the witness must stand outside the actor. We built that witness in software. The farmer sends soil; our platform reads not the guest list of who's there, but the live runtime of what they're doing — 17 AI agents, 221 models, already live, 97% disease detection.

5 · BRIDGE

Here's where everyone fails: 60–70% of single strains die in the field — a lab microbe dropped into drought is dropped into a warzone. We don't guess. We measure the battlefield first, then design the consortium built to survive it. Our published strains lifted nutrient uptake over 300% — peer-reviewed.

6 · BUSINESS MODEL

The value splits cleanly. The farmer gets the yield — a target of 20–25% more, a third less fertiliser. The producer pays us to prove and tailor his product. We sell the eyes; they sell the muscle. And Cyro Cavalcante — biofertilizer in 35 countries — just signed to white-label through us.

7 · TRACTION

It's real today: five signed LOIs, a 1,200-hectare German cooperative — and when Europe freezes, our AI trains on live summer fields in Chile. We retrain every six months, not once a year. We bend time.

8–9 · SCALE

Because the witness is universal, soil is only the first system. The engine underneath — our agentic layer, 1,500 commits — already builds other ventures, from lab science to construction. We're not building an agent. We're cultivating systems that build systems. Soil is the first machine off the line.

10 · TEAM

Built by the scientist who invented the method, a Nature Communications lead author, the engineer who built the platform, and an operator with 120 LATAM agri-deals. Ruhr-University Bochum, EXIST-funded.

11 · VALIDATION

We're honest. Patent-pending, not granted. Greenhouse proof today; field next. R² 0.68 — the 625-metagenome trial across three climate zones funds 0.85. We fund the proof; we don't assume it.

12 · ASK

So the ask: €1.5 million — to buy an industry its eyes. [pause] We didn't invent this rule. We translated the oldest one there is. Read the system before you change it. Thank you.

🌱 Platform persona — Farmer the Cultivator

~70s · grounded, soil/seasons/yield.

"Your field already speaks — you just haven't been able to hear it. For generations we judged soil by its chemistry: nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium. But that's the label on the jar, not the life inside it. Beyond Biomes reads that life — what the microbes in your soil are actually doing — and hands you back a living app for your field: what's missing, what to plant next, and the exact biological mix to bring it back to health. You send a sample; we diagnose, we prescribe, you treat. Then every season your field teaches the system, and the next recommendation gets sharper. The goal: more yield, less fertiliser, healthier ground to hand to your children. We read the system — before we change it."

🔬 Platform persona — Scientist the Observer

~75s · rigorous, evidence, out-of-band.

"You know the gap between the bench and the field — a strain that thrives on a plate gets out-competed in real soil. Beyond Biomes closes that gap with observation, not assumption. We read function — metagenomics and expression — not just taxonomy: who's present matters less than what they're doing. Every result is a receipt: R², p-values, an out-of-band record you can reproduce and publish. Our consortia come from a multi-objective optimizer, not a hunch; our 625-metagenome program spans three climate zones. And we're honest about where we stand — R² 0.68 today, funding the path to 0.85. Collaborate on our open platform: your knowledge becomes a prediction a farmer can act on tomorrow — and a co-authored result for you."

🏭 Platform persona — Producer the Builder

~70s · practical, durable, production-grade.

"You live with one number: how often your product fails in the field. Up to 70% of single strains don't survive contact with real soil — and a product that fails once never sells again. Beyond Biomes is the boring layer that makes your product reliable. We profile the field first, validate where your formulation actually works, and tailor it to that exact stress environment — so you sell proof, not promises. White-label our diagnostics, plug your portfolio into our API, and let every treated field feed back evidence that compounds. Production-grade, defensible, durable. We give you the eyes; you sell the muscle."
