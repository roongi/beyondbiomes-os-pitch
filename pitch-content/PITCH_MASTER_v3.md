# Beyond Biomes — Master 3-Min Pitch · v3 ("Nature → AI-native")
**Folds in the personality** (warm Socratic microbiologist; The Witness; "eyes before muscles"; Living Blueprint) on top of v2's honesty + fixes. ~480 words · 3:00 · 12-slide-locked. A distinct variant for the listen/skip/decide set (v3 leads with the *nature-reliability thesis*; v2 leads soil-first).

**[SLIDE 1 · TITLE]**
"What if the most advanced AI we're building already had a **3.5-billion-year-old blueprint**? I'm Dr. Ringo Schwabe — a microbiologist. Everyone in AI is racing to build stronger **muscles**, and Gartner says **over forty percent of those agent projects will be cancelled by 2027** — because they're **blind**. Nature never made that mistake: it built the **eyes before the muscles**. Our rule is one line: **read the system before you change it.**"

**[SLIDE 2 · PROBLEM]**
"Take the oldest system we farm — the soil. We still read a living microbiome as flat chemistry: nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium. That's like **debugging software by weighing the hardware** — you learn what's *there*, never what's *missing*. So billions in biological products get applied **blind**."

**[SLIDE 3 · LANDSCAPE]**
"And three people stand around every field who've never been in the same conversation. The **farmer**, who knows his land by hand but can't see the biology beneath it. The **scientist**, who knows what's failing but is nowhere near the field. The **producer**, who sells the cure and hopes it survives."

**[SLIDE 4 · TECHNOLOGY]**
"Beyond Biomes connects them — by borrowing nature's oldest trick. In a living cell, the enzyme that copies DNA **never grades its own work**; a separate proofreader checks it. Nature decided **the witness must stand outside the actor.** We built that witness in software. The farmer sends soil; our platform reads not the **guest list** of who's there, but the **live runtime** of what they're doing — **17 AI agents, 221 models, already live, disease detection at 97 percent.**"

**[SLIDE 5 · BRIDGE]**
"Here's where everyone fails: **sixty to seventy percent of single strains die in the field** — a lab microbe dropped into drought is dropped into a warzone. We don't *guess*. We measure the battlefield first, then design the consortium built to survive it. Our published strains lifted nutrient uptake **over three hundred percent** — peer-reviewed."

**[SLIDE 6 · BUSINESS MODEL]**
"The value splits cleanly. The **farmer** gets the yield — a *target* of twenty to twenty-five percent more, a third less fertiliser. The **producer pays us** to prove and tailor his product. **We sell the eyes; they sell the muscle.** And **Cyro Cavalcante — biofertilizer in thirty-five countries — just signed to white-label through us.**"

**[SLIDE 7 · TRACTION]**
"It's real today: **five signed LOIs**, a **1,200-hectare** German cooperative — and when Europe freezes, our AI trains on live summer fields in **Chile**. We retrain **every six months, not once a year. We bend time.**"

**[SLIDE 8–9 · SCALE]**
"And because the witness is **universal**, soil is only the **first** system. The engine underneath — our **agentic layer, fifteen hundred commits** — already builds other ventures, from lab science to construction. We're not building an agent. We're **cultivating systems that build systems.** Soil is the first machine off the line."

**[SLIDE 10 · TEAM]**
"Built by the scientist who **invented** the method, a **Nature Communications** lead author, the engineer who built the platform, and an operator with **120** LATAM agri-deals. Ruhr-University Bochum, EXIST-funded."

**[SLIDE 11 · VALIDATION]**
"And we're honest. **Patent-pending, not granted.** Greenhouse proof today; field next. R-squared **0.68** — the **625-metagenome** trial across three climate zones funds **0.85**. We fund the proof; we don't assume it."

**[SLIDE 12 · ASK]**
"So the ask: **1.5 million euros — to buy an industry its eyes.** [pause] We didn't invent this rule. We translated the oldest one there is. **Read the system before you change it.** Thank you."

**Voice note (from PERSONALITY_NARRATIVE):** deliver as *perspective offered, not prescribed* — warm, certain, unhurried; you're a scientist letting them in on a 3.5-billion-year-old secret, not selling.
