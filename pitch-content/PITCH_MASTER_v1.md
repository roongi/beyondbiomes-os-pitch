## THE CLEAN SCRIPT (what you say)

**[SLIDE 1 · TITLE]**
"I'm Dr. Ringo Schwabe — and what a time to be alive. The machines around us get smarter every month, and the whole world is racing for the best model. But Gartner says **over forty percent of AI projects will be cancelled by 2027** — not because the models are weak. Because they're **blind**. So our rule is one line: **eyes before muscle. Read the system before you change it.**"

**[SLIDE 2 · PROBLEM]**
"Start with the oldest system we farm — the soil. The whole industry treats a living microbiome as flat chemistry: nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium, pH. That tells you what's *there* — never what's *missing*. So billions in biological products get applied **blind**."

**[SLIDE 3 · LANDSCAPE]**
"And three people stand around every field who've never been in the same conversation. The **farmer** — who knows his land by hand, but can't see the biology beneath it. The **scientist** — who knows exactly what's failing, but is nowhere near the field. The **producer** — who sells a cure, and prays it works."

**[SLIDE 4 · TECHNOLOGY]**
"Beyond Biomes puts all three in one loop. The farmer sends soil; our platform reads not *who* is living in it, but *what they're doing* — **seventeen AI agents, two hundred twenty-one models**, running on our own servers for **thirty euros a month**. And it's already live: fifteen thousand real samples, crop-disease detection at **ninety-seven percent**. Like a doctor for the soil — we diagnose, prescribe, and treat."

**[SLIDE 5 · BRIDGE]**
"Here's the part everyone fails at. **Sixty to seventy percent of single strains die in the field.** We don't *guess* — we measure the field's stress first, then design the exact living consortium built to survive it. Our published strains lifted nutrient uptake **over three hundred percent** — peer-reviewed."

**[SLIDE 6 · BUSINESS MODEL]**
"And the value splits cleanly. The **farmer** gets the yield — a *target* of twenty to twenty-five percent more, with a third less fertiliser. The **producer pays us** to prove and tailor his product. One of them — **Dr. Cyro Cavalcante, who sells biofertilizer in thirty-five countries** — just signed to white-label through us. And every paid field sends data back that sharpens the next."

**[SLIDE 7 · TRACTION]**
"This is real today. **Five signed letters of intent.** A twelve-hundred-hectare German cooperative. And because we run fields in **both hemispheres** — winter here, summer there — our system re-trains **every six months, not once a year.** We bend time."

**[SLIDE 8–9 · MOAT / WHY THIS SCALES]**
"But soil is only the **first** system. The engine underneath it — our **agentic layer** — is a system that *builds* systems: **fifteen hundred commits** of infrastructure that has already spun up four other businesses, from lab science to construction. Soil is the first machine off the line. The same factory builds the next."

**[SLIDE 10 · TEAM]**
"The team to do it: the scientist who **invented** the method, a **Nature Communications** lead author, the engineer who built the platform, and an operator who's closed **a hundred and twenty** agri-deals in Latin America. Out of Ruhr-University Bochum, EXIST-funded."

**[SLIDE 11 · VALIDATION]**
"And we're honest about proof. Our model is at **R-squared zero-point-six-eight** today. This round funds the exact **six-hundred-metagenome** trial — across Germany, Chile and Spain — to reach the **zero-point-eight-five** field standard. **We fund the proof. We don't assume it.**"

**[SLIDE 12 · ASK]**
"So the ask: **one-and-a-half million euros — to buy an industry its eyes.** [pause] Read the system before you change it. Thank you."
