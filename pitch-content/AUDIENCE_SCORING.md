## What each audience loves (trust trigger) → lead visual
- **AI builders (Skool / Early AI-dopters):** the *gap map itself* — it names what they circle but haven't built, then shows nature already solved it. Peer-to-peer, "perspective offered, not prescribed." **Lead: m_clean → m_pairing.** Trust trigger: their own words reflected back (the receipt quotes).
- **VC / investor:** "observability is the moat; the agentic layer closes every gap = a category, not a feature." **Lead: m_hero (scroll-stopper) → m_bb (the layer closes every gap).** Trust trigger: defensibility + the social-proof quotes (13 experts confirm the bottleneck).
- **Scientist:** the biological rigor, each mechanism correct. **Lead: m_pairing.** Trust trigger: proofreading polymerase / immunity / quorum sensing stated precisely.
- **Farmer / producer:** the meta agentic layer is *too high-level* — they want "doctor for the soil." **Lead: NOT these maps — use page1 + the cycle.** Trust trigger: yield, less fertiliser, a real product. (Keep the gap map out of the farmer flow.)
- **Banker / grant jury:** "demos that work until they don't" vs production; reliability first. **Lead: m_pairing → m_bb.** Trust trigger: honesty (patent-pending, targets), the boring-layer discipline.
