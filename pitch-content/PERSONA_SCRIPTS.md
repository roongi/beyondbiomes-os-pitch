# Beyond Biomes — Platform Persona Scripts (English) + Video Storyboards
_For the app: a ~60-75s explainer per user type, in the persona voice from PERSONALITY_NARRATIVE. Honesty rules apply (patent-pending, yield=target, greenhouse proof). Each has a Veo (Nano Banana 2 / veo-3.1) storyboard for the "living" platform video._

---
## 1. FARMER — voice: the CULTIVATOR (grounded, soil/seasons/yield)
**Script (~70s):**
"Your field already speaks — you just haven't been able to hear it. For generations we judged soil by its chemistry: nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium. But that's the label on the jar, not the life inside it. Beyond Biomes reads that life — what the microbes in your soil are actually *doing* — and hands you back a living app for your field: what's missing, what to plant next, and the exact biological mix to bring it back to health. You send a sample; we diagnose, we prescribe, you treat. Then every season your field teaches the system, and the next recommendation gets sharper. The goal: more yield, less fertiliser, healthier ground to hand to your children. We read the system — before we change it."
**Veo storyboard:** (a) farmer's hand in soil at golden hour; (b) phone showing a calm soil "diagnosis" rising from the field; (c) a greener field next season. Tone: warm, patient, hopeful.
**Do:** soil, seasons, roots, resilience, yield. **Don't:** speed/hype. **Signature:** substrate, cultivate, rhizosphere, ecosystem.

---
## 2. SCIENTIST — voice: the OBSERVER (rigorous, evidence, out-of-band)
**Script (~75s):**
"You know the gap between the bench and the field — a strain that thrives on a plate gets out-competed in real soil. Beyond Biomes closes that gap with observation, not assumption. We read *function* — metagenomics and expression — not just taxonomy: who's present matters less than what they're doing. Every result is a receipt: R-squared, p-values, an out-of-band record you can reproduce and publish. Our consortia come from a multi-objective optimizer, not a hunch; our 625-metagenome program spans three climate zones. And we're honest about where we stand — R-squared 0.68 today, funding the path to 0.85. Collaborate on our open platform: your knowledge becomes a prediction a farmer can act on tomorrow — and a co-authored result for you."
**Veo storyboard:** (a) microscope → DNA/expression data resolving into a clean network; (b) a "receipt" card with R²/p-value; (c) two researchers across hemispheres connected by a data thread. Tone: precise, calm, credible.
**Do:** observe, verify, reconcile, deterministic, receipts. **Don't:** dogma/arrogance. **Signature:** out-of-band, function, reproducible.

---
## 3. PRODUCER — voice: the BUILDER (practical, durable, production-grade)
**Script (~70s):**
"You live with one number: how often your product fails in the field. Up to seventy percent of single strains don't survive contact with real soil — and a product that fails once never sells again. Beyond Biomes is the boring layer that makes your product reliable. We profile the field first, validate where your formulation actually works, and tailor it to that exact stress environment — so you sell *proof*, not promises. White-label our diagnostics, plug your portfolio into our API, and let every treated field feed back evidence that compounds. Production-grade, defensible, durable. We give you the eyes; you sell the muscle."
**Veo storyboard:** (a) product bottles on a line; (b) a field map showing "validated zones" lighting up; (c) a handshake/white-label cartouche. Tone: confident, commercial, grounded.
**Do:** durable, production-grade, survives contact, the boring layer. **Don't:** magic/hype. **Signature:** validate, de-risk, white-label, API.
