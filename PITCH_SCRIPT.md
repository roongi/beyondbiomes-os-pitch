# Beyond Biomes OS — 3-Minute Pitch Script

**Audience:** IDEA / Bochum ecosystem jury — Wirtschaftsentwicklung, VC, Volksbank Sprockhövel (MD), Aulinger (law firm), Bryck.
**Focus:** the business model & the OS. Soil = the funded, regulated proof.
**Total run-time:** ≈ 3:00 at ~150 wpm. The webapp speaks this automatically (browser voice); use this page as your teleprompter for a live read.

> Delivery: warm, certain, unhurried. Land the last word of each line, then move. Pause 1 beat at every "—".

---

### [0:00–0:15] · SLIDE 1 — Title
**Say:** "Beyond Biomes. We learned to read the language of living soil — then turned that method into an operating system that lets a single operator run an entire business. Here's the whole idea in three minutes."
*On screen:* logo + "The OS for the 1000× operator."
*Delivery:* open calm and confident; this is a promise, not a hook.

### [0:15–0:40] · SLIDE 2 — The method (the engine)
**Say:** "Start with the method, because it's the engine. Every system that survives in nature obeys one rule: never let a process grade its own work. DNA is proofread by a separate enzyme. The immune system checks against a reference no intruder can edit. We build software the same way — eyes before muscle: observe, verify against reality, then act."
*Delivery:* this is your differentiation — slow down on "never let a process grade its own work."

### [0:40–1:08] · SLIDE 3 — The output: the 1000× operator
**Say:** "The output of that method is leverage. Beyond Biomes OS wraps any workflow in an agentic harness — agents do the work, an independent witness proves what actually happened. One operator, with the OS, runs what used to take a whole team. We call it the thousand-x operator — not more headcount, more verified output per person. Watch: every action leaves a receipt the operator never has to write."
*On screen:* the live simulator — click **＋ Deploy a vertical** once or twice; the multiplier climbs, receipts tick.

### [1:08–1:33] · SLIDE 4 — One market: soil
**Say:** "We proved it in one market first: soil. Europe's biostimulant market is one-point-eight billion dollars today, growing past three billion by 2030 — and EU regulation just opened the door. Our diagnostic, RhizoGenie, reads what the microbes actually do. Biomizer turns that into a treatment. Real product, real market, real regulation."
*Delivery:* this is the credibility beat for the VC and the banker — say the numbers crisply.

### [1:33–1:55] · SLIDE 5 — The federation
**Say:** "Soil is only the first vertical. The same method drops into construction, facilities, and education — each run by an operator, each sharing one brain: the Holding. It carries the reliability layer, the data, and the playbook, so every new vertical launches faster and cheaper than the last."

### [1:55–2:15] · SLIDE 6 — Business model
**Say:** "The model is simple and capital-efficient. Diagnostics start at three hundred euros a month. Audits, five to fifteen thousand. Retainers, one to five thousand a month — recurring. Local-first infrastructure keeps costs near zero, so a single operator can carry one to two million in revenue."
*Delivery:* the banker's slide — steady, factual.

### [2:15–2:33] · SLIDE 7 — Market signal
**Say:** "And the demand is already speaking. Builders and operators who saw the method told us the same thing, unprompted: production systems need verification, not just automation. That's not our claim — it's the market naming the exact gap we fill."

### [2:33–2:47] · SLIDE 8 — Team
**Say:** "We're a federation of operators ourselves — soil science, infrastructure, construction, facilities, and education, across three continents. Each runs a vertical. Each makes the whole stronger."

### [2:47–3:02] · SLIDE 9 — The ask
**Say:** "We're raising to finish the product, hire two engineers, and launch the next three verticals. Beyond Biomes: read the living system, prove it, then scale it. Let's talk."
*On screen:* switch the funding tab to match the room — €750K (default) · €500K · €1.5M · Traction-first.
